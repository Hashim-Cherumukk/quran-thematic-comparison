Thematic Comparison

Stinginess vs Abundance

Ma’un criticizes those who withhold small kindness. Kauthar opens by declaring divine abundance.

Heedless Prayer vs Sincere Worship

Ma’un describes showy, careless prayer. Kauthar commands worship with sincerity and sacrifice.

Ostentation vs Devotion

Ma’un condemns doing worship to be seen. Kauthar purifies intention toward Allah alone.

Withholding vs Sacrifice

Ma’un ends by noting even petty aid is withheld. Kauthar ends by commanding noble, generous sacrifice.
